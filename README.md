# Upanishad
Make the transformative wisdom of the Upanishads with meaning accessible to all sincere seekers worldwide, fostering spiritual growth, intellectual inquiry and collective human evolution through collaborative knowledge management.
Welcome to the Upanishads Repository - a comprehensive, open-source digital archive dedicated to preserving, organizing, and making accessible the ancient Vedantic wisdom contained in the Upanishads.
This project represents a global collaborative effort to:
Preserve ancient Vedantic knowledge in a structured, searchable format
Collect the Basic Slokas, Meaning of the Upanishads from Research Scholars 
Enable sincere seekers of truth of life understand wisdom of Upanishads
Facilitate global discussion and deeper understanding of Upanishads
Reward contributors with innovative BITAO system
